Welcome to POLLAREZ

Pollarez is a free online website for all to access and roam around in. Known as the ultimate school slack off tool, Pollarez provides many different functions, varying from games, to beautiful scenery, to panic buttons!

Tread carefully though; we don't want the website to be taken down because you got caught!

Our Mission

We understand that school can feel like a drag. We understand how the time seems to stand still when you're listening to your teacher blab on constantly without break. We get it.

For all students who wished they were anywhere except in the classroom, or simply know the content already; Pollarez helps quench the feeling of boredom in anyone!

We also have some additional perks, such as a Study Zone (for people who actually want to learn) and a PANIC Button for those who have had enough!
